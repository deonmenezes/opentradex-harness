Every serious trader ends up juggling ten tabs.

A TradingView chart. Three exchange terminals. A half-broken Polymarket script. A Slack channel full of signals nobody has time to read. OpenTradex is the command cockpit that replaces all of it. Local-first, paper-by-default, and fully yours. 

scan markets  →  AI filters signal  →  you approve  →  paper fill  →  review

Built for the way real traders actually work.

◈

Houston-style cockpit

Three-pane dark terminal. Positions on the left, AI chat in the center, live feed on the right. Resizable, keyboard-first, calibrated for 8-hour sessions.

⚙

One harness, every market

Polymarket, Kalshi, TradingView, Alpaca, IBKR, Binance, Coinbase, OANDA, MetaTrader, Dukascopy, Robinhood, DraftKings. Drop a connector file, the harness auto-discovers it.

⚡

Bring your own AI

Anthropic, Google, any OpenAI-compatible endpoint, or local CLIs. Route reasoning, speed, and orchestration to different models. Your keys, your choice.

⛑

Paper-first safety

Live trading is gated behind a 24-hour lock and an explicit mode flip. Kelly sizing, daily-loss caps, kill switches, and a panic button that flattens everything.

✸

x402 agentic payments

Let the agent auto-pay 402-gated APIs with USDC micropayments on Base. Generate a testnet wallet in the dashboard, set a per-call cap, done.

⌁

Your keys, your machine

Everything lives in ~/.opentradex/config.json. No SaaS, no account, no telemetry. Works offline for paper trading. Run it on a VM and drive it from your phone.

12 connectors, one dashboard.

Prediction markets, equities, crypto, forex, and sportsbooks. Add your keys once — switch between rails from the same chat window.

Designed for traders who want one surface for everything.

Prediction-market arbitrage

Watch Kalshi and Polymarket side-by-side. Ask the AI to flag any event that's priced >3% apart across both rails.

Cross-asset news trades

Stream Reuters, Bloomberg, CoinDesk, and FT. Tag each headline with tickers and let the AI propose the best paper trade under your risk cap.

Automated scanner loops

Cron the harness every 1–30 minutes. It scans markets, filters by confidence, and surfaces only setups that clear your thresholds.

Paper-to-live graduation

Start paper-only, review your P&L for a week, then flip the 24-hour lock if you like what you see. Same cockpit, same commands.

Agentic data purchases

Let the AI pay for premium data feeds on-demand with USDC. Every micropayment is logged with tx hash in the ledger — no subscriptions you forget about.

Remote control

Run the harness on a VM, drive it from your laptop or phone via the mobile app. Bearer-token auth, LAN or Cloudflare tunnel.
